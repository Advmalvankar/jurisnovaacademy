Legal Intern (Remote – Full-Time)

We are offering internship and training opportunities for law students looking to gain practical experience in the legal field.

As a Legal Intern, you will coordinate with fellow interns, review their work, and assist them with queries related to assigned tasks. You will also play an active role in conducting live sessions, where you will explain tasks and concepts using presentations (PPTs).

In addition, you will support the legal team through research, drafting, and case analysis. This role is designed to develop your leadership, communication, and legal skills through hands-on training and mentorship.
